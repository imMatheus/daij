The acceleration is achieved through 7 layers that all get progressively denser across 6 sections:

- **Accelerating pulse**: hi-hat doubles each section (1 -> 2 -> 4 -> 8 -> 16 per bar)
- **Drums**: sparse kick evolves into double-kick/snare patterns with fills
- **Bass**: sustained whole notes compress into rapid triplet figures with increasing distortion
- **Chords**: slow string pads transform into fast rhythmic e-piano stabs
- **Melody**: isolated notes become full cascading pentatonic runs with octave jumps
- **Drone**: enters halfway and builds in harmonic complexity and brightness
- **Outro**: everything fades to nothing simultaneously
