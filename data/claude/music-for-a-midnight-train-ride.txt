**"Midnight Rail"** — Downtempo Ambient Jazz, 76 BPM, 80 bars (~2:32)

**Layers (8 voices):**

2. **Hi-hats** — 16th-note clacking with perlin-modulated gain, evoking train wheels on tracks
3. **Kick/snare** — swung broken beat with `bd ~ [~ bd] ~` syncopation mimicking the train's rocking motion
4. **Acoustic bass** — deep rhythmic pulse like the engine rumbling beneath
5. **Electric piano chords** — dreamy offbeat voicings with delay, like city lights through foggy windows
6. **Piano melody** — contemplative Bb minor pentatonic phrases, sparse and reflective
7. **Clarinet counter-melody** — lonely, distant lines appearing only in the chorus and bridge sections, with vibrato
8. **Sub bass drone** — deep sine pad cycling through the chord roots, the constant hum of the rails

**Structure:** Intro builds from just vinyl + sparse kick → verse adds bass and chords → chorus brings full drums, clarinet, and brighter filters → verse 2 pulls back with random gain drops → bridge strips to half-time with wide reverb → outro fades the kick to nothing over strings.
