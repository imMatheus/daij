The 5:4 polyrhythm is layered at multiple levels:
- **Percussion**: kick in `bd*4` against rimshot in `rim*5`
- **Pitched**: bass plays 4-note root patterns per cycle while melody plays 5-note pentatonic runs
- **Structure**: the rim voice uses `.every(5, ...)` modifiers to create meta-fives
