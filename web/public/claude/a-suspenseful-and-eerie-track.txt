- **Ticking hi-hats** with perlin-modulated gain — nervous, unpredictable energy that accelerates from 4x to 8x to 16x
- **Phrygian mode** throughout — the b2 (Db) gives that characteristic dark, menacing color
- **Dissonant string pads** with Cm7→Db^7→Abm7→Bbm7 cycling through slow filter sweeps
- **Haunting melody** with lots of rests, heavy delay (.5:.4:.6), and vibrato — notes appear and dissolve
- **Ghostly high texture** on gm_epiano2 only during climax sections, with massive reverb/delay
